About Bible Coding Academy

A faith-driven digital skills initiative empowering young people across Africa.

Our Story

Bible Coding Academy (BCA) was founded to bridge the gap between digital skills and spiritual growth. We believe young people can become purpose-driven innovators when equipped with both technical knowledge and strong Christian values.

Starting from INES-Ruhengeri and local churches, BCA is expanding across Rwanda and Africa through partnerships, workshops, and community-driven programs.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To train young people in coding and digital literacy while grounding them in Christian values, producing purpose-driven digital innovators.

Vision

A generation of Christian innovators building tools that serve the Church and society.

Meet the Founder

Patrick Hans Weedy Jr.

Patrick is a software engineer, youth leader, and visionary behind Pcodefaith and Bible Coding Academy. His passion for technology and ministry inspired him to create a platform where young people can grow spiritually while gaining practical digital skills.

With experience in children’s ministry, software development, and community training, Patrick leads BCA with a heart for impact and transformation.
